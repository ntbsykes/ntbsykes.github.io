Rockfish Genomics

Population genetics and sex chromosome evolution

Northeast Pacific Rockfish Studies

At the University of Victoria 2021-Current

In the Owens lab, we are part of a group which assembles researchers from the University of Victoria, the University of California, Berkeley, Fisheries and Oceans Canada, and the U.S. National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. Together, we study the unique evolution, local adaptation, and population dynamics of Sebastes rockfishes to inform conservation efforts and to shed light on the forces and variation underlying speciation.
